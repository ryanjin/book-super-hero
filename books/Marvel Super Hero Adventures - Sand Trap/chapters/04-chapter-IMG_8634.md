## 中文演绎
蜘蛛侠

彼得·帕克原本只是个普通男孩，直到他被一只放射性蜘蛛咬到，变成了神奇蜘蛛侠！他拥有超强力量，能爬墙，也能跳出惊人的距离。作为一个很有科学头脑的孩子，彼得还亲手做出了自己的蛛网发射器。彼得非常认真对待自己作为超级英雄的职责，因为本叔叔曾教导他一句话：能力越大，责任越大。

## 英文原文朗读
Spider-Man

Peter Parker was just a normal kid when he was bitten by a radioactive spider and became The Amazing Spider-Man! He has super strength, can climb walls, and can jump incredible distances. Being the science-minded kid that he is, Peter also made his very own web-shooters. Peter takes his job as a Super Hero seriously because of the lesson his Uncle Ben taught him: With great power comes great responsibility.
